How to Play Trivia Game

Objective:
Your goal is simple: answer as many trivia questions correctly as you can to rack up points and claim the highest score!
Setup:
This is a single-player game.
You’ll face 10 multiple-choice questions based on your chosen category.
Questions will appear one at a time.
Rules:
Each question has a 30-second time limit.
If you don’t answer within the time limit, it’ll be marked as incorrect.
Points are awarded for each correct answer.
No points are given for incorrect answers.
External help (like using the internet or books) are not allowed.
Once you submit an answer, you can’t change it.
Have fun, challenge yourself, and good luck!!!
